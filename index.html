DEF CON

In a joint team with LabRATs (MIT Lincoln Labs) and RPISEC, TechSec recently qualified to DEF CON! The Finals will take place July 27th - 30th at Caesar's Palace in Las Vegas. We're currently soliciting funding to pay for travel and hotel costs.

Also, the source code and binaries for the challenges to the qualifier round were just released. You can find writeups for most of the challenges on ctftime.
