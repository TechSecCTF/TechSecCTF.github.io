About Us

TechSec is MIT's premier security club. During the school year, we hold meetings approximately every week. During the fall semester we focus mostly on teaching security fundamentals. During the spring semester we focus mostly on competing in CTFs. If you're an MIT affliate, you can join our mailing list, techsec@mit.edu. You can also join our slack at techsec-mit.slack.com, which we mostly use for coordination during CTFs.
