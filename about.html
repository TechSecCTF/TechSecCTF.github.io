About Us

TechSec is MIT's newest (and only) security club. During the school year, we hold weekly meetings in 32-124 on Mondays at 6:30 PM. We also participate in several CTFs throughout the year. If you're an MIT affliate, you can join our mailing list, techsec@mit.edu. You can also join our slack at techsec-mit.slack.com, which we mostly use for coordination during CTFs.
